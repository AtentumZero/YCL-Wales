About

Welcome to the official website of the Welsh branch of the Young Communist League, the youth organisation of the Communist Party of Britain.

Founded in 1921, we are a revolutionary Marxist-Leninist organisation that advocates for the establishment of a socialist society in Wales and Britain as a whole.

We apply Communist theories and practice, many of which are summed up in our programme Britainâ€™s Road to Socialism, to organise and campaign effectively to overthrow state monopoly capitalism and transform Britain into a socialist society based on common ownership, democratic control, full equality, universal access to public services, environmental protection and advancement for the human race.

With members across the country, we actively engage in demonstrations, community work and direct action in communities across Wales to fight for the interests of working people and marginalised minorities.
